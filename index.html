About Me

Moving to the United States from South Korea at the age of one was a very 'difficult' decision. I literally had no idea what was happening. I moved to the suburbs of Rockland County at the end of elementary school then graduated from Villanova University in 2008 with a Bachelor's in History.

After graduating from Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, I hope to work as a full time developer. I enjoy playing pool, fishing, traveling, golf and whatever my friends want to do when we hang out.
